DYNAMICS EXPLORATION OF A 3DoF SYSTEM

Objective:

To understand Design and Analysis of Dynamics Exploration of a 3-Degree-of-Freedom(DoF) System


Apparatus used:

Quadcopter drone, Battery, Telemetry, Flight controller, Hover mounting system, Ball and socket joint.

  Description The three-degree-of-freedom hover system serves the purpose of instructing, demonstrating, and testing various types of drones. This system comprises a quadcopter drone and a dedicated mounting system. The mounting system possesses three degrees of freedom, whereas the drone itself has six degrees of freedom. The system effectively mitigates the additional degrees of freedom by orchestrating movement within special co-ordinates and results in the three-degree of freedom hover system.

Frame is the structure that holds all the components together. It supports the motors and other electronics and prevents them from vibrations. The frames are designed to be strong and lightweight. Motor An electric motor is an electrical machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy. Among these, brushless DC motors (BLDC) feature high efficiency and excellent controllability. Flight Controller a.k.a FC is the brain of the aircraft. It's a circuit board with a range of sensors that detect movement of the drone, as well as the user commands. Using this data, it then controls the speed of the motors to make the craft move as instructed.
